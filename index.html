I build systematic, multi-asset macro strategies, turning top-down macro into testable, risk-controlled allocation. The approach is macro-first: understand where the cycle sits before deciding what to own, and look for mechanisms, not patterns. Each position rests on a documented economic edge that survives a causal screen, not a signal mined from data. Risk comes before return: a hard cap binds the book across every regime, and the goal is to control the downside first. I treat overfitting as the claim to disprove, not defend, so a strategy earns real capital only after it holds up out-of-sample and, in time, live.
